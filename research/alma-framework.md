## What is ALMA?
**ALMA (Automated meta-Learning of Memory designs for Agentic systems)**

### Core Concept
- Meta-learns memory designs to **replace hand-engineered** designs
- Minimizes human effort
- Enables agentic systems to be **continual learners** across diverse domains

### How it works
- **Meta Agent** searches over memory designs
- Expressed as **executable code**
- **Open-ended** manner

## Relevance to ContextKeeper

| ALMA | ContextKeeper |
